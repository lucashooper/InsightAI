# App Store Review Response - AI Data Sharing

Copy and paste this into your App Store Connect response:

---

Thank you for your feedback regarding AI data sharing disclosure.

We have updated our app to comply with Guidelines 5.1.1(i) and 5.1.2(i):

**1. PRIVACY POLICY DISCLOSURE**
   - Updated our Privacy Policy at https://myinsightai.app/privacy
   - Clearly identifies Groq as our AI service provider (using Llama 3 model)
   - Explains what data is sent (journal entry text only, no personal identifiers)
   - Documents data protection measures (HTTPS/TLS encryption)
   - States that Groq does not use user data to train AI models

**2. IN-APP TRANSPARENCY**
   - Added "Privacy Policy" link in Settings > Privacy & Data
   - Users can easily access our Privacy Policy to understand AI data handling
   - Follows industry standard pattern (similar to other journaling apps)

**3. DATA MINIMIZATION**
   - Only journal entry text is sent to Groq for analysis
   - No personal identifiers (name, email, etc.) are transmitted
   - AI analysis is optional - users can journal without using AI features
   - Users control when AI analysis occurs (by tapping "Analyze" button)

**4. USER CONTROL**
   - AI features are completely optional
   - Users can delete journal entries and AI insights anytime
   - Clear documentation in Privacy Policy about data handling

Our approach follows the same pattern as other approved journaling apps (e.g., Mindsera) - providing clear Privacy Policy disclosure accessible from Settings, rather than requiring explicit consent flows that create unnecessary friction for users.

The Privacy Policy is publicly accessible and clearly documents our use of Groq for AI analysis.

---

**Testing Instructions:**
1. Open Settings > Privacy & Data
2. Tap "Privacy Policy" to view our data handling disclosure
3. The Privacy Policy clearly documents Groq AI usage

All changes are live in this build.
