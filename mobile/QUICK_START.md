```

› Press a │ open Android
› Press i │ open iOS simulator
› Press w │ open web

› Press r │ reload app
› Press m │ toggle menu
```

---

## 📱 How to Test

### Option 1: On Your Phone (Easiest)
1. Download **Expo Go** app from App Store or Play Store
2. Scan the QR code that appears
3. ✅ App opens on your phone!

### Option 2: Android Emulator
1. Press `a` in the terminal
2. Opens in Android Studio emulator

### Option 3: iOS Simulator (Mac only)
1. Press `i` in the terminal
2. Opens in Xcode simulator

### Option 4: Web Browser
1. Press `w` in the terminal
2. Opens in your browser
