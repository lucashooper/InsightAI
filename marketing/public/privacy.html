Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Insight ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered journaling application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide.

Journal Entries: All journal entries, notes, and personal reflections you create within the app are stored securely and encrypted.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, including features used, time spent, and general usage patterns to improve our service.

AI Analysis Data: When you use our AI analysis features, we process your journal entries to provide insights. This data is processed securely and never shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and maintain our AI journaling service
• Generate personalized insights and analysis
• Improve and optimize our application
• Communicate with you about updates and features
• Ensure the security of your account and data

4. Data Security

Your privacy is our top priority. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

• End-to-end encryption for all journal entries
• Secure data storage with encrypted databases
• Regular security audits and updates
• No third-party access to your personal journal content

5. AI Processing

When you use our AI analysis features, your journal entries are processed to generate insights. This processing:

• Occurs in secure, encrypted environments
• Is never used to train AI models on your personal data
• Does not share your content with third parties
• Can be disabled at any time in your settings

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and journal entries for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Delete your account and data
• Export your journal entries
• Opt-out of AI analysis features

8. Third-Party Services

We use select third-party services for:

• Authentication (secure login)
• Cloud storage (encrypted)
• Analytics (anonymized usage data only)

These services are carefully vetted and comply with strict privacy standards.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@myinsightai.app
