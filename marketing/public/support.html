If you have any questions about InsightAI, need help with your account, or want to report a bug, you can reach us using the options below.

Common issues

  • Can't sign in: Make sure you are using the same email address you used to create your account. If you still can't sign in, contact us and we can help reset access.
  • Data or sync problems: If entries are missing or not updating between devices, let us know which device and approximate time of the issue.
  • Billing questions: For questions about your subscription, include the email address linked to your InsightAI account and any relevant billing receipts.
